Why
Fusion Factory sprouted from an awareness that science is not simply a collection of words and facts in a textbook: it is a visceral, creative and constantly evolving set of information. Fusion Factory is a collection of workshops latching onto this principal, empowering secondary school students with a creative medium for them to grasp the reality and practicality of science. We constantly look forward, bringing into practice a new, and working, 21st century pedagogical model highlighting the outdated Victorian model which, gazing backwards, harmfully remains as the default for global education systems. The three-step thought process below represents the reasoning and positive consequential effects of Fusion Factory's philosophy.


Understanding the core basics of biology, physics and chemistry represents the path to intellectual creativity within the sciences. Early specialisation, common within global education systems, denies this.


Creative and intellectual freedom promotes scientific enquiry and a greater uptake in further science education.


A more diversely educated mind can sprout more diversely educated ideas to solve 21st century problems in a 21st century manner.
