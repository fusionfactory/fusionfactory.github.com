Fusion Factory is an educational charity founded by a group of science communicators which uses uniquely designed workshops and approach to make science learning an engaging, creative, accessible and enjoyable activity. We travel to schools presenting a real and functional 21st century educational model with the emphasis on accommodating the learner within scientific concepts, utilising their innate creativity as a means to learn and enrich the educational experience. We succeed in opening a portal through which learners are empowered to play with the idea of further study and career options within scientific fields. We are approachable, knowledgeable and down-to-earth communicators with a natural flair for identifying students' problem areas within science and inventing and delivering ingenious solutions, making them easier and fun to grasp.
