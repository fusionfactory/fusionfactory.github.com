Fusion Factory

Fusion Factory is a small start-up company, created by four science communicators, that uses uniquely designed workshops to make science learning an engaging, accessible, interactive and enjoyable activity. The emphasis is on accommodating the learner within the topic while tapping into their creativity, exploring roles in scientific teams and providing the learner with a memorable experience of being stuck-in. All this succeeds in providing a portal through which learners are empowered to seek out and play with the thought of a career or deep interest in science. We are approachable, knowledgeable, down-to-earth communicators with a flair for identifying problem areas in science subjects and inventing ingenious solutions to make them more easier and more fun to grasp.

Created by Luke Connolly, Rosie Ellis, Cian Ó Rathaile and Niamh Nic Aodha Bhuí.
